## 1️⃣ Objective

To evaluate brand-level pricing strategies by combining:

- Value Efficiency Index
- Price Ranking
- Revenue Contribution
- Pricing Gap Analysis

The goal is to identify whether brands are:

- Underpriced
- Premium-driven
- Revenue-dominant
- Misaligned (Need repositioning)

---

## 2️⃣ Methodology Framework

### Step 1: Value Efficiency Index
\[
Value\ Index = \frac{Performance\ Score}{Price}
\]

### Step 2: Ranking Logic
- Rank brands by Value Index
- Rank brands by Price
- Calculate Pricing Gap:

\[
Pricing\ Gap = Value\ Rank - Price\ Rank
\]

Positive Gap → Underpriced  
Negative Gap → Premium Strategy

## 5️⃣ Market Insight

The market is NOT purely value-driven.

Revenue leadership is influenced by:
- Brand strength
- Portfolio architecture
- Pricing segmentation
- Market positioning

Selective optimization is recommended.
Industry-wide discounting is not necessary.
